## Context / Non-Obvious Decisions
- The backend deliberately does **no cryptography** — it's a relay only. This is why the backend language decision (Node/TS) was made independently of the crypto library decision (Rust-based vodozemac) — they don't need to match, and don't.
- We are explicitly **not** using the Matrix network/federation — only Matrix's crypto primitives (vodozemac/matrix-sdk-crypto). This avoids the AGPL licensing issue that would come with libsignal, while keeping the backend fully custom and non-federated.
- No phone number requirement was a deliberate privacy differentiation choice, not a default — it adds real product work (contact discovery, account recovery) tracked as open questions in [[Projects/CyCove#Key decisions|Key decisions]] → Identity model and [[.claude/plan]].
